About Publiso

Introduction

Launched in 2022, Publiso is a popular ebook reading platform with a reputation for innovation, integrity and independence. We sell ebooks direct to millions of consumers around the world. You can read our ebooks online, paying for a cheap subscription charged monthly. Publiso is privately held and run by its founders, Akbar Ali and Badusha Basheer. It is the only independent ebook reading platform that available in every country in the world. Publiso hosts over a million unique ebook titles and has millions of members. In addition to our popular ebook-store, we provide services to book publishers. Publiso is a system that enables publishers to send secure ebooks to reviewers, instructors and readers.

Online (Browser-Based) Reader App

Our proprietary web-based reader enables desktop users to immediately start reading, without having to download or install software. Currently undergoing an upgrade to make it suitable for mobile platforms. Suitable for retail and library use.

The Team

Deep, broad, detailed domain expertise. The company comprises a small team of long-time ebook specialists. Our newest developer was engaged in 2022. Some of our guys started in 2020. It's fair to say that our team knows pretty much everything about processing, managing and securely delivering ebooks to a global audience.
